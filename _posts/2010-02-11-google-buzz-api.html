--- layout: post title: 'Google Buzz API' source: http://kinlane.com/2010/02/11/google-buzz-api/ domain: kinlane.com image: --- Over the next several months Google Buzz begin rolling out APIs for developers. Our plans involve full/read write support for updates with the Atom Publishing Protocol, rich activity notification with Activity Streams, delegated authorization with OAuth, federated comments and activities with Salmon, distributed profile and contact information with WebFinger, and much, much more. We invite developers to join us in these conversations on the Buzz API Group.
