--- layout: post title: 'Pay Per Action and Involvement' source: http://kinlane.com/2008/02/20/pay-per-action-and-involvement/ domain: kinlane.com image: --- Starting to see some ad networks and ad models moving beyond traditional views or clicks. Videoegg launched a new pay per engagement video model.

Advertisers pay only on an "engagement" basis or when a user rolls over the ad and the advertisers full screen is displayed.

It is a first for this type of true engagement model and really focuses your advertising.
