--- layout: post title: 'Google Chrome Embraces Flash Further' source: http://kinlane.com/2010/06/26/google-chrome-embraces-flash-further/ domain: kinlane.com image: http://kinlane-productions2.s3.amazonaws.com/google-chrome-logo.jpg --- Google releasde Google Chrome 5.0.375.86 on Thursday which includes integrated flash player being enabled by default. Interesting positioning by Google in the Flash v HTML5 battle.

Google Chrome is a leader in embracing HTML5 standards in their browser.

Now they are also further embracing Flash by allowing default integration and updates within Chrome. I think this is a wise move to encourage widespread adoption of Google Chrome as default browser.
