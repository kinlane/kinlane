--- layout: post title: 'Podcasting Using Your Phone' source: http://kinlane.com/2008/02/26/podcasting-using-your-phone/ domain: kinlane.com image: --- I was just playing with Cinch which is a new voice to RSS service. It allows you to call a number and it records a podcast for you. It is more focused, but similar to the voice translation service Jott that I have been using.

It gives you an RSS feed of the calls you make which you can easily blend into a Feedburner tracking RSS feed.

There is no limit to the recording unlike Jott, but of course it doesn't translate your calls.

Another valuable audio and podcasting web 2.0 service.
