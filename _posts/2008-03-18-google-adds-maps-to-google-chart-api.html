--- layout: post title: 'Google Adds Maps to Google Chart API' source: http://kinlane.com/2008/03/18/google-adds-maps-to-google-chart-api/ domain: kinlane.com image: --- Google released a new update for the Google Chart API. It allows you to create maps with specific data areas based upon what you pass.

You can highlight states, countries and other elements and display summary or other data regarding those elements.

A nice feature added to the API. The have a lot of features hitting the pavement lately across their web applications / API.
