--- layout: post title: 'RSS: < author > vs: < dc:creator >' source: http://kinlane.com/2010/06/27/rss--author--vs--dccreator-/ domain: kinlane.com image: --- I am always willing to admit when I'm wrong. I was just railing on a site for not providing <author> information in their RSS feed. I'm used to pulling the author name using the <author> tag.

I come to learn that <author> tag is just meant for the post author's email address. <dc:creator> is truly meant for the name of the author.

I didn't know this. I had to Google it and I came up with this post explaining RSS: <author> vs: <dc:creator>.
