--- layout: post title: 'Mobile Editing of Google Spreadsheets in 45 Languages' source: http://kinlane.com/2011/03/02/mobile-editing-of-google-spreadsheets-in-45-languages/ domain: kinlane.com image: http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-sRbuM8hc-5E/TW5nBf4LniI/AAAAAAAAAw0/hEmmYXoqGNQ/s320/nexus-screenshot-2.jpg --- Google has enabled many of the same mobile editing capabilities in Google Docs, for Google Spreadsheets.

You can now edit Google Spreadsheets in 45 languages on Android, IPhone and IPad devices.

Google has aslo added eight separate print sizes in Google spreadsheets, including tabloid, statement, executive, folio and A3, A5, B4 and B5.

Some new features that continue adding to Google Docs viability as a powerful publishing platform.
