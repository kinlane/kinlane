--- layout: post title: 'iPhone Case is a Polaroid Printer' source: http://kinlane.com/2011/05/02/iphone-case-is-a-polaroid-printer/ domain: kinlane.com image: http://kinlane-productions2.s3.amazonaws.com/mimeo/other/Iphone-Polaroid-Print.jpg --- As technology rolls forward, there are many new concepts, but sometimes things we know from the past can also be reborn.

A new concept IPhone case from Mac Funamizu prints photos just like a Poloraoid.

Using a QR code and geo tagged map the printed picture can also be linked to a digital version on the web.

A ery intriguing concept that merges new and old into a very usable piece of technology.
